About NExDI Tech

From one idea in Borno to impact thousands across Africa

The Founder's Story

My name is Ndara yunana dawara, and I founded NExDI Tech because I saw two things clearly growing up in Nigeria:

First — the incredible talent and hunger to learn among young people. Second — the massive gap between that potential and real opportunity. I watched brilliant minds drop out of school or settle for jobs far below their ability because they lacked access to modern digital skills, mentorship, tools, and networks.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To become Africa’s leading hub for digital innovation — where every young person has the tools, knowledge, and confidence to shape the future.

Mission: To deliver world-class technology solutions, provide practical digital education, and build an inclusive ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is NExDI Tech? +

NExDI Tech is a youth-focused digital innovation hub in maiduguri, Nigeria. We provide hands-on training in AI, robotics, software development, digital skills, internships, and tech consultation.

Who can join our programs? +

Anyone passionate about tech — secondary school students, undergraduates, graduates, career changers, and even organizations looking to upskill teams. No prior experience required for most beginner programs.

Are your programs free or paid? +

We offer free community workshops and bootcamps, affordable paid programs (₦50,000–₦150,000), and custom enterprise packages. Scholarships and payment plans are available for qualifying students.

Do you offer remote or online training? +

Yes! Most programs are hybrid — online classes + optional in-person sessions in Ibadan. Fully remote options are available for many courses and internships.

How can I partner with NExDI Tech? +

Schools, NGOs, startups, and companies can collaborate for training, internships, workshops, or tech consulting. Reach out via our contact page — we love partnerships!
