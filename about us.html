About NExDI Tech

From one idea in Borno to impact thousands across Africa

The Founder's Story

My name is Ndara, and I founded NExDI Tech because I saw two things clearly growing up in Nigeria:

First — the incredible talent and hunger to learn among young people. Second — the massive gap between that potential and real opportunity. I watched brilliant minds drop out of school or settle for jobs far below their ability because they lacked access to modern digital skills, mentorship, tools, and networks.

In 2023, after working in tech startups and seeing how fast the world was moving, I decided it was time to stop waiting for change to come from outside. I returned to Ibadan with a simple but bold vision: build a company that doesn't just talk about innovation — it creates it, teaches it, and makes it accessible to anyone willing to work hard.

The early days were tough. We started with one laptop, borrowed projectors, and free community halls. We ran our first coding and robotics workshops for secondary school students — many of whom had never touched a computer before. Seeing their eyes light up when their code made a robot move for the first time reminded me why I started this.

From those early days of free community trainings, we grew into a real company. We began delivering paid software projects, robotics solutions, and structured internship programs. We partnered with schools, NGOs, and local businesses. Every success story — a student landing their first remote job, a small business launching their app — fueled the next step.

Today, NExDI Tech stands as a symbol of what’s possible when talent meets opportunity. We’ve trained hundreds, built real-world solutions, and created a space where young Nigerians can dream big and actually achieve it. But we are just beginning.

This is not just a company. It is a movement — to prove that geography, background, or money should never limit what a Nigerian youth can achieve in the digital age.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To become Africa’s leading hub for digital innovation — where every young person has the tools, knowledge, and confidence to shape the future.

Mission: To deliver world-class technology solutions, provide practical digital education, and build an inclusive ecosystem that turns talent into real opportunity.
