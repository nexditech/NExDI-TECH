Big Announcement from NExDI

We are excited to announce the launch of our brand-new Digital Innovation Hub in Nigeria!

This hub is designed to create a collaborative space where youths, entrepreneurs, and innovators can learn, build, and scale their ideas. It features a co-working space, training facilities, and mentorship programs.

Through this hub, NExDI will provide resources in AI, Cloud, Cybersecurity, and Data Analytics. Our goal is to make Nigeria a central hub for digital innovation in Africa.

Cybersecurity Training Bootcamp

NExDI is empowering students with hands-on cybersecurity skills through a specialized bootcamp...

The bootcamp covered ethical hacking, penetration testing, and incident response. Students worked on simulated labs to practice real-world defense strategies.

Many participants have already secured internships and freelance jobs after the program.

Partnership with Tech Giants

NExDI is proud to announce partnerships with top technology companies including Microsoft, Google, and AWS.

These partnerships open doors for students to access free resources, cloud credits, certifications, and mentorship from global experts.

Our learners can now take certification exams, access professional-grade tools, and join exclusive competitions.

AI & Innovation Summit

NExDI recently hosted the AI & Innovation Summit 2025, bringing together over 500 participants including students, startups, and industry leaders.

The summit featured keynote sessions on AI in healthcare, agriculture, and fintech, plus workshops where participants built real-world AI solutions in 24 hours.

The event ended with a pitch competition, where the winning team secured funding and mentorship for their AI startup.
